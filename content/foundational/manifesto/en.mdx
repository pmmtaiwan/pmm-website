# Our Manifesto

<CanonicalSlogan>Good Music. Period.</CanonicalSlogan>

<InstitutionalIdentity>
Philharmonia Moments Musicaux

A Chamber Orchestra from Taiwan.

A home for music that deserves to be heard.
</InstitutionalIdentity>

In a world driven by spectacles, algorithms, fleeting trends, and vanishing attention spans, we represent an alternative.

We believe music transcends entertainment. It is humanity's most profound form of communication.

We refine the essential.

We pursue uncompromising quality.

We speak with music itself.

Because good music deserves nothing less.
